What it does

A player that gets out of the way.

Everything here serves one goal: your media, playing smoothly, wherever you are — without asking you to babysit it.

Recovers on its own

A network hiccup used to mean a spinner and a restart. Suvo detects the drop, reconnects, and resumes from where the stream stalled — automatically.

Everywhere you watch

The same app on your phone, tablet, desktop, and the big screen — with a proper 10-foot interface built for your TV remote.

Picks up where you left off

Resume positions, watch history, and favorites follow you. Start on the couch, finish on the train — sign in and it all syncs.

Organized automatically

Your Live, Movies, and Series sort themselves out — with cover artwork and metadata so your library actually looks like one.

Yours, and private

Local-first by default. Sync is optional. No ads, no tracking, no profiling, and we never sell your data — full stop.

Bring your own playlist

Point Suvo at your Xtream Codes account or an M3U URL. Suvo supplies no content of its own — it plays what's yours.

The resilient core

Buffering is a bug,
not a fact of life.

Most players give up the moment the network wobbles. Suvo treats a dropped stream as a state to recover from — a small engine watches playback and walks it back to health, so you stay in your seat.
