Support

Help getting started, and how to reach us.

Suvo is a media player for playlists you already own. You bring your own Xtream Codes account or M3U URL; the app provides no content of its own.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and add a playlist — enter your Xtream host/username/password, or paste your M3U URL.
  2. Your Live, Movies, and Series appear automatically.
  3. Pick something to play; the app remembers where you left off.

Common questions

Where do I get a playlist?

From your own media/playlist provider. Suvo does not supply or sell playlists or content.

Playback keeps stopping.

This usually points to your network or provider. Suvo automatically retries and recovers from short network drops — if it persists, check your connection and that your playlist is still active with your provider.

How do I sync across devices?

Sign in; your favorites, history, and playlists sync via your account. Without an account, everything stays on the device you’re using.

How do I delete my data?

Clear it in the app or uninstall to remove local data. To delete account data, email us and we’ll handle the request. See the Privacy Policy.
