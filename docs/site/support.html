Lumen Player — Support

Lumen Player is a media player for playlists you already own. You bring your own Xtream Codes account or M3U URL; the app provides no content of its own.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and add a playlist — enter your Xtream host/username/password, or paste your M3U URL.
  2. Your Live, Movies, and Series appear automatically.
  3. Pick something to play; the app remembers where you left off.

Common questions

Where do I get a playlist? From your own media/playlist provider. Lumen Player does not supply or sell playlists or content.

Playback keeps stopping. This usually points to your network or provider. Lumen automatically retries and recovers from short network drops.

How do I sync across devices? Sign in; your favorites, history, and playlists sync via your account.
