Privacy Policy

Lumen Player · Last updated 8 July 2026

Lumen Player (“the app”) is a generic media player. You supply your own media playlist (an Xtream Codes account or an M3U URL); the app bundles no content of its own and neither hosts nor provides any channels, streams, or media. This policy explains what limited data the app handles and why.

Data you provide

  • Playlist connection details — the Xtream host/username/password or M3U URL you enter. Used only to fetch your playlist and play your media. Stored on your device and, if you sign in, in your account so it syncs across your devices.
  • Account (optional) — if you create one, an email/identifier is used to authenticate you and sync your data.

Data the app creates

  • Favorites, watch history, and resume positions — kept so the app remembers what you watched and where you left off.
  • Device bindings — a device record so your account can sync across the devices you use.

How it is stored

Without an account, all of the above stays on your device. If you sign in, this data is stored in our sync backend (Supabase) solely to make it available on your other devices. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use it for advertising or profiling.

Streaming and third parties

When you play media, the app connects directly to the playlist provider you configured. Your traffic and credentials go to that provider under their own terms and privacy policy — not to us. The app has no control over, and takes no responsibility for, the content or practices of playlist providers you choose.

Data retention and deletion

Local data is removed when you clear it in the app or uninstall. Account data is deleted on request or when you delete your account. To request deletion, contact us (see below).

Children

The app is a general-purpose media player and is not directed at children. Because playlist content is user-supplied, its suitability is outside the app’s control.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date above will change accordingly.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@lumenplayer.app.
