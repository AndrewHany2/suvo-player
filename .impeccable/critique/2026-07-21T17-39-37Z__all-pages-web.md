# Priority Issues

**[P1] One save concept wears three identities: Live channels use a heart glyph, movie/series detail uses a star labeled Add to Favorites/Saved, and Home shelves label the same collection My List.**
- Why: Three names + two icons for one mental model break Recognition and Consistency; a first-timer cannot tell hearting a channel and starring a movie land in the same list. The star also collides with rating-gold stars.
- Fix: Pick one verb (Save / My List) and one glyph across all three surfaces; prefer a bookmark/plus over the star to avoid the rating-gold collision.
- Command: $impeccable clarify — LiveTVScreen.web.jsx:122-135,126; MovieDetail.web.jsx:284-294; HistoryScreen.web.jsx:436

**[P1] Three Back buttons: MoviesScreen uses Button variant=ghost icon=back; SeriesScreen hand-rolls a surface2 pill with accent text; Movie/Series detail hand-roll a floating rgba(0,0,0,0.55) pill.**
- Why: Same escape action rendered in three visual languages — the most-used control on drill-in screens is the least consistent. B's detector independently flagged the rgba(0,0,0,0.55) pill literal at HistoryScreen.web.jsx:216.
- Fix: Standardize on the Button variant=ghost icon=back primitive everywhere; keep only the over-art scrim variant where it must sit on a backdrop.
- Command: $impeccable distill — MoviesScreen.web.jsx:37; SeriesScreen.web.jsx:258-273; MovieDetail.web.jsx:124-147

**[P2] Destructive removes have no undo or confirm: card close buttons call removeFromMyList / removeFromWatchHistory immediately.**
- Why: A mis-tap silently deletes a resume position; with cross-device sync it is gone everywhere. Violates Error Prevention/Recovery on an easy-to-hit target.
- Fix: Add an inline Removed · Undo snackbar (5s) rather than a modal confirm.
- Command: $impeccable harden — HistoryScreen.web.jsx:468,526

**[P2] Single-Light + decorative-motion drift on the LIVE indicator: .suvo-live-dot paints resting indigo #6C5CE7 text plus a blinking dot, and the favorited heart sits at resting indigo.**
- Why: Single-Light reserves saturated accent for active-path/focus only; a persistent indigo blink at rest is both accent-at-rest and decorative motion (a product ban). DESIGN reserves success green for online/live state.
- Fix: Move the LIVE indicator to success/steel with no blink (or gate the blink out on web as on TV); keep the favorited heart on the neutral ladder with indigo reserved for the pressed/active tick.
- Command: $impeccable colorize — AppNavigator.web.jsx:111-118; LiveTVScreen.web.jsx:128

**[P2] Off-palette hardcoded placeholder: SeriesScreen sets placeholderTextColor=#555 while the identical Movies input uses colors.faint.**
- Why: #555 is off the steel/slate ladder and violates the Literal-Hex-Sync rule; two sibling screens render the same field differently. Notably, B's palette detector did NOT catch this one — A found it by source read — so it is a real residual beyond the detector's coverage.
- Fix: Replace #555 with colors.faint.
- Command: $impeccable polish — SeriesScreen.web.jsx:293; MoviesScreen.web.jsx:45

**[P3] Help is thin outside the player: only the player exposes its shortcut legend; browse screens have no discoverable shortcuts or contextual help.**
- Why: Power users cannot find accelerators and mid-browse users have no guidance.
- Fix: Add a /-to-search accelerator hint and lightweight tooltips on the shelf title / see-all affordance.
- Command: $impeccable document — VideoPlayerScreen.web.jsx:933-935
